Before you ask

Things you probably want to know

Is a refurbished Leap V2 actually worth ₹22,999?

Honestly, only you can answer that. Here's the math: a new one is ₹1,03,900. A sketchy OLX listing is ₹12–18k with no warranty and no shipping. Ours is the same chair Steelcase built — with new wear parts and a real warranty — at roughly a quarter of new. If you sit 6+ hours a day, the back-pain math works out fast.

How is this different from buying on OLX?

Three things. One, we replace the parts that actually fail before we ship — new cylinder, new casters, mechanism serviced. Two, we offer a 6-month warranty; OLX sellers don't. Three, we ship anywhere in India insured, instead of you driving across town to inspect a chair.

Where do you get the chairs?

From offices in India that are upgrading their furniture, and from coworking spaces winding down. We don't import containers of unknown chairs. Every chair has a verifiable workplace history.

How old will my chair be?

Most are between 3 and 7 years old. We don't accept chairs over 8 years old. The Leap V2 frame is warrantied by Steelcase for 12 years, so even a 7-year-old chair is structurally past its midpoint, not its end.

What does "standard refurbishment" include?

New gas cylinder, new casters, foam inspection and replacement where needed, recline mechanism disassembly and re-lubrication, upholstery extracted-cleaned twice, every one of the 11 adjustments tested by hand, sign-off by a second technician.
